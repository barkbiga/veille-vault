## Résumé

Evan Greenberg (CEO Chubb) Q1 2026 earnings call : stratégie digitale inchangée malgré surge AI capabilities, mais **"agentics"** et enterprise AI = thèmes émergents prioritaires. Citation clé : "You can't just be listening to others. You got to have firsthand knowledge. Otherwise, you yourself start to become irrelevant." Signal leadership : passer de l'observation à l'expérimentation pour éviter obsolescence. Industrie assurance encore early-stage sur opérationnalisation des agents IA. Attente = lower costs, simplified workflows via LLM enterprise tools. **Signal faible** sur vocabulaire "agentics" adopté par Fortune 500 insurance.

## 📝 Notes personnelles

**Context** :
- Chubb = global P&C + life insurance, $50B+ revenue
- Greenberg = CEO long-tenured, track record digital transformation
- Q1 2026 earnings call = communication publique, donc message calibré (pas détails techniques)

**Terme "agentics"** :
- Pas "AI agents" mais "**agentics**" → jargon émergent ou positioning marketing ?
- Possible origine : "agentic AI" (AI systems with agency, autonomy, goal-seeking)
- Adoption C-suite insurance = signal maturité discussion (vs. hype "AI will transform everything")

**Stratégie Chubb** :
- "Digital strategy remains unchanged, execution on track" → continuité rassurante (pas pivot chaotic)
- Mais "level of attention" sur agentics augmente → priorisation ressources
- "What's still emerging is how agentics actually operationalize" → admission d'incertitude (healthy)

**Philosophie leadership** :
- "You can't just be listening to others. You got to have firsthand knowledge."
- Implication : Chubb expérimente en interne (pas juste vendor demos)
- "Otherwise, you yourself start to become irrelevant. So as a leader, all that's on my mind."
- **Contrarian faible** : Beaucoup de CEOs disent ça, peu agissent

**Attentes enterprise AI** :
- Large model developers → years of investment → usable enterprise tools
- Tools mature → lower costs, simplify workflows
- **Question** : Quels workflows assurance visés ?

  - Underwriting (risk scoring, document extraction, policy recommendation)
  - Customer service (chatbots → agentic assistants with tool use)
  - Compliance (regulatory filing, audit trail automation)
