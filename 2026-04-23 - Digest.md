### ⭐ 4.0 — IAM — [Why Liveness Checks and Facial Matching Are Not the Same Thing](https://blog.spruceid.com/digitalidentity-why-liveness-checks-and-facial-matching-are-not-the-same-thing-and-why-the-difference-matters/) ⚠️
**Spruce ID** · 2026-04-22

Clarification conceptuelle identity proofing : **liveness detection** (anti-spoofing, on-device éphémère) ≠ **facial matching** (compare live vs. stored ID, server-side retention). Privacy implications radicalement différentes. NIST IAL2 permet facial matching mais ne l'impose pas. Risque policy : confusion → mandats disproportionnés ou gaps sécurité. Architecture recommandée : liveness on-device + facial matching encrypted <24h retention.

**Tags** : `Identity-Proofing` `NIST` `IAL` `Biometrics` `Data-Minimization` `Privacy-by-Design` `Government-ID` `eIDAS`
